Page Purpose

Index Page- This page gives you a welcome to the individual that you are being introduced to. It gives you a taste of Tiffany's personality and an idea of how to view her as you go on to the other pages. It also gives you to means of contact. A link is provided to take you to the social media page and when you click on the image of the individual it gives an email address for contact.
Personality Page- Gives an in-depth view of Tiffany's character and values as she relate to her academic life. This is where most of her personality will showcased.
Professional Page- This is where you will be introduced to Tiffany on a professional level. You will find a copy of her resume and her future career pursuits.
Personal Development Page- Chapters 4 – 7 of “Twelve Pillars” by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener were summarized and reflected on. It can be seen how Tiffany has made a connection with the things read to her academic life.
Story Board Page- An overview can be found of the purpose of all the pages and what are the features and themes used to create the webpages along with the wireframe design used.
Reference Page- A list of all the resources used to acquire the necessary knowledge to complete this website can be found here.

Theme

The theme is warm and welcoming. Cornsilk and a warm orange were used to create a vibrant yet comforting atmosphere. The text size ,color and font was also very calm and elegant. White and dark purple was used while the font was modern and clean.
