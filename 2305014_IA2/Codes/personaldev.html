Insight from Twelve Pillars by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener(Chapters 4-7)

Summary and Reflection

Chapter 4: Achieve Your Goals

Within this chapter, it highlights how important it is to set goals. It helped me to realize the true benefit of setting goals. Reaching them is great but allowing your goals to impact your growth is even better. You will need to have a great level of determination. Applying this mindset will allow me to set clear goals for improving myself and my academic life.

Chapter 5: The Proper Use of Time

This chapter shows us how to use time wisely. We may get a lot of opportunities but one of those opportunities is more important than the rest. This analogy allowed me to apply this to my everyday life by focusing on what is more important maybe even seeing the bigger picture and not just what is in front of you.

Chapter 6: Surround Yourself with the Best People

Jim Rohn mentions that you are the average of the five people you spend the most time with. Therefore this shows the importance of the people you surround yourself with on a daily. If you surround yourself with people that feed you with negativity then you will never take some of the greatest opportunities that may come your way. Surround yourself with people who allow you to grow, motivate you, and inspire you.

Chapter 7: Be a Life-Long Learner

This chapter starts by my mentioning that "Formal education will make you a living. Self-education will make you a fortune." This was very interesting. We see where some people believe that they have learned all they need to know after acquiring a degree or even a doctorate but with that type of mindset then they will never grow. For you to be a lifelong learner you must continue to seek knowledge, whether it is by reading books or even observations. Life is about improvement and growth.
