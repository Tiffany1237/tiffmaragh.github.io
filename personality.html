Impact On My School Life

Trait:
Persistence
Impact:

Persistence has made and continues to greatly impact my academic life. No matter how challenging an assignment or course may be, I always strive to complete it to the best of my ability. Remindering the goal I have set for myself allows me to stay focused during exams, be involved in university committees, and helps me manage my time effectively. This persistence also drives me to improve and keep learning, despite setbacks or obstacles constantly.

Emotion:
Optimism
Impact:

Feeling overwhelmed with attending university full time and also working full time comes naturally, my optimism keeps me grounded. I remind myself that all the hard work will pay off in the end, which helps me maintain a positive mindset in stressful times. This emotional strength has led me to push through staying up in the night for extended hours after long hours of work and school to finish coursework, committee assignments, and study for exams. This in turn allows me to maintain my GPA.
